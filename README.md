# Praneet Koppula

**AI design experiments for products, tools, and agent workflows.**

I am a product design and UX leader based in San Francisco. I use small, working experiments to explore how people understand, direct, and evaluate AI systems - especially when the work is complex, consequential, or still taking shape.

My background spans product design, UX research, service design, and design leadership. Here, I am interested in making design judgment more visible, testable, and useful inside the tools that build software.

## What I am exploring

- How design judgment can become part of coding-agent workflows without being flattened into a checklist.
- How AI products can expose context, uncertainty, provenance, and proposed action clearly enough for people to stay in control.
- How agents can support UX research and product discovery while preserving human interpretation and review.
- What small experiments can reveal before a team commits to platform-scale architecture.

## Selected experiments

- **[GStack Port for Codex](https://github.com/mphaxise/gstack-port-for-codex)** - Codex-native planning, review, design, QA, and release workflows, adapted with explicit runtime and compatibility boundaries.
- **[AI Agent UX Research Platform](https://github.com/mphaxise/ai-agent-ux-research-platform)** - Research and design probes into UX validation, agent experience observability, evidence calibration, and human-review boundaries.
- **[Design Skill Pack for AI Agent Coding Platforms](https://github.com/mphaxise/design-skill-pack-for-ai-agent-coding-platforms)** - Experiments in giving coding agents stronger UX, product-discovery, and research practices.
- **[GitHub PR Map](https://github.com/mphaxise/gh-pr-map)** - A small working tool that turns repository contribution data into an interactive geographic view.
