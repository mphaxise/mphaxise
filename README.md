# Praneet Koppula

**Product design leader building AI-native experiences for complex, expert workflows.**

I am a San Francisco–based product design and UX leader who works as a player-coach: shaping product direction, developing teams and design practices, and staying close to research, prototypes, critique, and implementation.

Over a decade at MathWorks, I progressed from senior individual contributor to UX leader across sophisticated engineering products and workflows. Today, I explore how people understand, direct, review, and collaborate with AI agents—and how product teams can bring stronger design judgment into agent-assisted development.

My GitHub contains working experiments in agent UX, AI-assisted product development, design-quality workflows, research tooling, and developer experience.

## What I am exploring

- How design judgment can become part of coding-agent workflows without being flattened into a checklist.
- How AI products can expose context, uncertainty, provenance, and proposed action clearly enough for people to stay in control.
- How agents can support UX research and product discovery while preserving human interpretation and review.
- What small experiments can reveal before a team commits to platform-scale architecture.
